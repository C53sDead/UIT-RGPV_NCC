About UIT-RGPV NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at University Institute of Technology (UIT), Rajiv Gandhi Prodhyougiki Vishvavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal, is a vibrant and disciplined unit that fosters leadership, patriotism, and selfless service among students. We are affiliated under the 12 MP Battalion Unit, Bhopal Group and proudly contribute to the national movement of youth empowerment through uniformed training and civic responsibility.

Our Objectives

  • Develop leadership and character in youth.
  • Instill the value of unity and discipline.
  • Promote a sense of patriotic duty and responsibility.
  • Encourage participation in social service and community work.
  • Promote fitness and endurance through training and outdoor activities.

Vision

To create a pool of organized, trained, and motivated youth with leadership qualities in all walks of life who will serve the nation regardless of the career they choose.

Achievements

Our cadets have participated in numerous camps including Republic Day Camp (RDC),All India Thal Sainik Camp(AITSC), Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Camp (EBSB), and more. Our alumni have joined the armed forces and other prestigious services, bringing pride to our company and institute.

Message from the ANO

Lt. (Dr.) Kaustubh Dwivedi

Associate NCC Officer, UIT-RGPV NCC

“Discipline, leadership, and service to the nation are not just ideals — they are the foundation of true character. As the ANO of this proud unit, I strive to nurture these qualities in each cadet, preparing them to serve with honor and integrity.”
