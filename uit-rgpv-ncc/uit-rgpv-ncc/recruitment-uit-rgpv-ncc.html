NCC Recruitment 2025

We are excited to welcome new cadets to the NCC Unit of UIT-RGPV. Below are the details regarding this year's recruitment process.

📅 Date of Recruitment

Written Test & Physical Screening: 15th July 2025

Location: College Football Ground

📝 Syllabus for Written Test

  • General Knowledge (India, Defence, NCC)
  • Current Affairs (Last 6 Months)
  • Basic English & Reasoning

💪 Physical Fitness Requirements

  • Running: 1.6 km in 7 minutes (boys), 9 minutes (girls)
  • Pushups: 20 (boys), 10 (girls)
  • Plank Hold: Minimum 1 minute
